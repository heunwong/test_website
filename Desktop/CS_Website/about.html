About Crimson Scholars

Crimson Scholars is here to help, in more ways than one. Gone are the old days of looking for a local tutor and the hassle of commuting for either one of you. We help give you access to a network of highly qualified tutors all over the US that are ready to help. Started in 2014 by Huen Wong, Crimson Scholars is rapidly growing to be the premiere tutoring choice for student around the US. With our proprietary online interface system, you and your tutor are able to do everything you could in a face-to-face session, but from the comfort of your own homes or wherever you chose.

Being sucessful with a tutor is also about fit. With our technology you'll be connected to our vast network of professionals and increase your chances of finding someone that will really help and take you to the next level. What are you waiting for? Register today and we can help set you up with the perfect tutor.

Meet The Team

Hugh (Heun) Wong - Founder

Heun started his passion for tutoring while in college. He loves to help others through critical thinking and learning skills. He started the company to help students to gain access to tutors all around the country by not being geographically limited to a local few. He attended Harvard University and hold a B.S. in Psychology.
